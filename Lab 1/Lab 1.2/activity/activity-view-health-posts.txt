@startuml

|User|
start

:User initiates a request to view health posts;
|System|
:Retrieve health posts from the database;
:Display health posts to the user;

|User|
if (User wants to search for health posts) then (Search Post)
  :User provides search criteria;
  |System|
  :Retrieve filtered health posts from the database;
  :Display search results to the user;
else (Read Post)
  :User selects a health post to view;
  |System|
  :Retrieve the selected health post from the database;
  :Display the health post to the user;
endif

|User|
stop

@enduml
